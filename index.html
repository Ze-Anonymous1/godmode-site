How GOD MODE uses your Google data

GOD MODE only accesses your Google account after you explicitly grant permission on Google's consent screen. Here is exactly what we request and why.

  • ●Gmail — read your messages. So GOD MODE can summarise your inbox, understand context, and draft accurate replies for you.
  • ●Gmail — send & draft messages. So GOD MODE can create drafts and send the emails and follow-ups you approve, on your behalf.
  • ●Google Calendar — read events. So GOD MODE can find availability and help schedule meetings around your existing commitments.
  • ●Basic profile & email address. So GOD MODE knows which account it is acting for and can sign you in.

GOD MODE uses this access only to provide the features above. Your Google data is never sold, never used for advertising, and never used to train AI models. You can disconnect GOD MODE from your Google account at any time. See our Privacy Policy for full detail on how data is handled, stored and deleted.
