Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how GOD MODE (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, stores and protects your information — including any data we access through Google Auth and the Google APIs when you connect your Google account.

1. Who we are

GOD MODE is an automation platform that helps business operators manage their email, calendar and daily admin. When you sign in with Google and grant permission, our system acts on the tasks you approve. Contact us any time at divinemarketers@gmail.com.

2. Information we access

  • Google account data — with your explicit permission on Google’s consent screen, we access your Gmail messages, the ability to draft and send email, your Google Calendar events, and your basic profile and email address.
  • Content you provide — instructions, approvals and settings you give the platform.
  • Technical data — basic security and error logs needed to run the service.

3. How we use it

  • Reading and summarising your inbox so you know what needs attention.
  • Drafting and sending the emails and follow-ups you approve.
  • Reading your calendar to find availability and help schedule meetings.
  • Identifying which account we are acting for and signing you in.

4. Google API Limited Use disclosure

GOD MODE’s use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Specifically, we do not use Google user data to serve advertisements, we do not sell Google user data, and we do not use it to train generalised AI or machine-learning models. Human access to your Google data is not permitted except with your explicit consent, for security purposes, to comply with applicable law, or in aggregated and anonymised form.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We share it only with the service providers strictly required to run the platform (for example, secure cloud hosting), and only to the extent needed to deliver the features above. These providers are bound to protect your data and act only on our instructions.

6. Storage, retention & deletion

Your data is stored securely and encrypted in transit. We retain it only as long as needed to provide the service. You can disconnect us from your Google account at any time via your Google Account permissions, and you can request deletion of your data by emailing us — on disconnection or request, we delete the associated data from our systems.

7. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards, including encryption in transit and access controls, and limit access to only what is necessary.

8. Your rights & NZ Privacy Act

Consistent with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information, and you may withdraw permission at any time. To exercise these rights, email divinemarketers@gmail.com.

9. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time; material changes will be reflected here with a new “Last updated” date.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email divinemarketers@gmail.com.
